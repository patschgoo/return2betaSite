# AuthMe

## What It Does
AuthMe protects your account by requiring you to register a password and log in each time you connect. This prevents other players from impersonating you.

## How It Affects You
- On first join, you must register with a password.
- Each time you connect, you must log in within 90 seconds or you will be timed out.
- Until you log in, you cannot chat, use commands (except `/login` and `/register`), or walk around.
- Usernames must be 3–20 characters and can only contain letters, numbers, and underscores.
- If you are not registered, you will be directed to the server's registration page.

## Commands
| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/register <password>` | Register your account with a password. |
| `/login <password>` | Log in to your account. |
| `/logout` | Log out of your account. |
| `/changepassword <old> <new>` | Change your password. |
| `/unregister <password>` | Unregister your account. |
