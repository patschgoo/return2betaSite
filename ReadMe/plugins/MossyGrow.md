# MossyGrow

## What It Does
MossyGrow makes cobblestone slowly turn into mossy cobblestone when placed near water or existing mossy cobblestone, adding a natural aging effect to builds.

## How It Affects You
This is a passive, automatic feature. Your cobblestone builds will gradually become mossy over time.

## How It Works
- **Near water:** Cobblestone within 1 block of water converts to mossy cobblestone after **5 minutes**.
- **Near mossy cobblestone:** Cobblestone within 1 block of mossy cobblestone converts after **3 minutes**.
- The spread is slow and gradual, creating a natural overgrown look.

## Notes
- No commands — this happens automatically.
- If you don't want mossy cobblestone, keep your builds away from water and existing mossy blocks.
