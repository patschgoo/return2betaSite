# NoFullHealthEating

## What It Does
NoFullHealthEating prevents you from eating food when your health and hunger are already full, saving your food from being wasted.

## How It Affects You
If your health is already full, right-clicking with food won't consume it. This prevents accidentally eating food you don't need.

## Notes
- No commands — this works automatically.
- A quality-of-life improvement to prevent food waste.
