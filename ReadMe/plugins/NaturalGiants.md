# NaturalGiants

## What It Does
NaturalGiants adds giant zombies as rare, powerful hostile mobs that naturally spawn in the world.

## How It Affects You
You may encounter giant zombie mobs in the world. They are extremely dangerous but drop valuable loot.

## Giant Stats
- **Health:** 200 HP (100 hearts — very tough!)
- **Spawn rate:** 1% chance to spawn
- **Damage:** 10 HP per hit (5 hearts)
- **Fire attack:** Giants have a 25% chance to launch 5 fireballs with an 8-block range, setting you on fire for 3 seconds.
- **Burns during day:** Yes — giants catch fire in daylight.

## Drops
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Bone | Up to 12 |
| Diamond | Up to 3 |

## Tips
- Giants are extremely rare but very dangerous. Fight them in groups!
- They burn during the day, so encountering them at dawn can give you an advantage.
- The fire attack is their most dangerous ability — keep your distance.
- Killing a giant can be a quest objective (see QuestPlus).
