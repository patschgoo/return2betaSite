# Citizens

## What It Does
Citizens adds NPCs (Non-Player Characters) to the world. These are interactive characters that can talk to you, trade, heal, give quests, and more.

## How It Affects You
You can interact with NPCs around the server. They may serve as quest givers, healers, blacksmiths, traders, or simply chat with you.

## NPC Types & Interactions

### Talking NPCs
- Walk near an NPC and they may say things like "Hello", "How are you today?", or "Having a nice day?"
- Right-click an NPC with a **Book** (ID 340) to interact or talk.

### Healers
- Healers can restore your health for a fee: **$100** or **10 Yellow Flowers**.
- Right-click a healer with a **Gold Hoe** (or the appropriate item) to heal.

### Blacksmiths
- Blacksmiths repair your tools and armor for a fee.
- Repair costs vary by material (e.g., Wood tools: $0.25, Diamond tools: $1.25).

### Traders
- Traders buy and sell items. Right-click to open their trade window.

### Quest Givers (Questers)
- Some NPCs offer quests (see QuestPlus readme).

### Wizards
- Wizards can teleport you for a fee: **$100** or **10 Yellow Flowers**.
- Interact with a wizard using a **Feather** (ID 288).

## Tips
- NPCs appear as regular players with names displayed above their heads in white.
- You can select an NPC by right-clicking with the select item (any item, `*`).
- NPC names and locations are set by the server — you can interact but not create or modify NPCs.
