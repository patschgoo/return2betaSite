# DeadMansChest

## What It Does
DeadMansChest automatically creates a chest at your death location containing all your items, so you don't lose them to despawning or lava.

## How It Affects You
Members have access to death chests. When you die, your items are placed in a chest at the spot where you died.

## Features
- A **chest spawns automatically** at your death location with all your dropped items inside.
- A **sign is placed on the chest** showing your name and death info.
- A **death message** is broadcast: *"[player] died. Deploying death chest."*
- Death chests are **automatically removed after 150 seconds** (2.5 minutes), dropping the remaining items on the ground.
- If you die in liquid (water/lava), the chest replaces the liquid so your items don't burn or float away.

## Tips
- Rush back to your death location quickly — the chest only lasts 2.5 minutes!
- Once you pick up your items, the chest will be removed.
- The chest cannot be looted by other players during the timer.
- Items that fall from mining the chest itself also drop normally.
