# DynamicLight

## What It Does
DynamicLight makes held items that emit light (torches, glowstone, lava buckets, etc.) illuminate the area around you in real-time, even without placing them.

## How It Affects You
Hold a light-emitting item in your hand and the area around you will light up as you walk. Great for exploring caves without placing torches everywhere.

## Light Items
| Item | Brightness |
|------|-----------|
| Torch | Full |
| Glowstone | Full |
| Jack o' Lantern | Full |
| Lava Bucket | Full |
| Fire | Full |
| Lava | Full |
| Portal Block | Medium |
| Redstone Torch | Medium |
| Redstone Repeater | Dim |

## Notes
- No commands — just hold a light source item.
- The light follows you as you move.
- Placing the item removes the dynamic light (the placed block lights up normally).
- This is visual only — it does not prevent mob spawning.
