# Pickup

## What It Does
Pickup automatically collects dropped items when you walk near them. No need to look directly at items or stand on top of them.

## How It Affects You
When you're near dropped items on the ground, they will automatically fly into your inventory. This works for all item types.

## Features
- Items are picked up just by walking nearby — no interaction needed.
- Works for all dropped items: mined blocks, mob drops, player drops, etc.
- Useful for mining, farming, and mob grinding — saves time collecting items.

## Notes
- No commands needed — this runs automatically.
- If your inventory is full, items will stay on the ground as normal.
