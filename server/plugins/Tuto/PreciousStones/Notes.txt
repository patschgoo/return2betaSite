§6=== PreciousStones - Important Notes ===
§fSneaking (Shift) bypasses damage fields.
Mushrooms, flowers, fire, crops bypass protection.
§cChests, crafting tables, furnaces CANNOT be protected
by PreciousStones. Use LWC for those instead!
§fFields from different players cannot overlap.
§cIf you don't log in for 45 days, fields get purged.
