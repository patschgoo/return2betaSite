§6=== ItemLWC - Lock Expiration ===
§cALL lock types expire after 30 days of inactivity!
This includes private, password, AND public locks.
§cALL block types can expire (chests, furnaces, doors, etc).
§fUse a §eClock§f on a locked block to check time remaining.
§fTo remove your lock: sneak-punch your protected block
and choose the remove option from the menu.
