§6=== ItemLWC - Protection Types ===
§ePUBLIC §f- Anyone can see the item, nobody can take it.
Great for display cases. Visitors can create these too.
§ePRIVATE §f- Only YOU can access the item. Members only.
§ePASSWORD §f- Locked with a password you set. Others use
an Iron Ingot on the block to enter the password.
