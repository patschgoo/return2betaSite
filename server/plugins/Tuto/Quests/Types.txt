§6=== Quests - Quest Types ===
§eCollect §f- Gather items (cobble, wheat, diamonds...)
§eKill Monsters §f- Zombies, skeletons, creepers, ghasts...
§eKill Animals §f- Chickens, cows, pigs, wolves...
§eMine Ores §f- Coal, iron, gold, diamond, obsidian...
§ePlace Blocks §f- Dirt, cobble, planks, bricks...
§eKill Players §f- PvP bounties
§eMulti-Objective §f- Several tasks at once (25% chance!)
Multi-objectives can include killing giants or humans.
