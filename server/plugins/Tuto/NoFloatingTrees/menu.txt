§6=== NoFloatingTrees ===
When you break the bottom log of a tree, any
floating treetop left behind is automatically
removed! No more ugly floating leaves and logs
stuck in the sky.
§6Details:
§eLeaf removal radius: §f4 blocks around logs
§eRemoval style: §fGradual (1-5 seconds per block)
§eInitial delay: §f15 seconds after bottom log breaks
§eMax tree size: §f200 blocks
§fWorks together with §eTimber§f — sneak-chop
the base to fell the whole tree at once.
