§6=== LWC Tips ===
§fLockable blocks: Chests, Furnaces, Dispensers,
Signs, Wooden/Iron Doors, Trapdoors.
§eMagnet: §fDropped items within 3 blocks get sucked
into your locked chests. Great for mob grinders!
§eIron Doors: §fClick to open if you have access.
§eDouble Doors: §fBoth open together automatically.
§cRemember: Blocks do NOT auto-lock. Use /cprivate!
