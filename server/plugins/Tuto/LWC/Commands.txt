§6=== LWC Commands ===
§e/cprivate §f- Lock a block (only you can use it)
§e/cpublic §f- Public lock (anyone uses, only you break)
§e/cpassword <pass> §f- Password-protect a block
§e/cunlock <pass> §f- Unlock a password-protected block
§e/cremove §f- Remove your lock from a block
§e/cmodify <player> §f- Add a player to your lock
§e/cmodify -<player> §f- Remove a player from your lock
§e/cinfo §f- Check who owns a locked block
§e/climits §f- See how many locks you have
